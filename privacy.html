AutoClic

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 2026

Overview

AutoClic is a free, open-source auto-clicker application for Windows. This policy explains what data AutoClic accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

AutoClic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No data is sent to any server or third party.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting is included.
  • No account or registration is required.

Keyboard and Mouse Input

AutoClic uses global keyboard and mouse hooks (pynput) solely to:

  • Detect hotkey presses configured by the user (e.g. start/stop autoclick).
  • Simulate mouse clicks and key presses as directed by the user.
  • Record and replay mouse/keyboard macros when the user activates recording.

This input data is processed entirely on your local device and is never stored permanently or transmitted outside your computer. Macros are saved locally only when the user explicitly chooses to save them.

Local Storage

AutoClic stores configuration and profiles in: %APPDATA%\AutoClic Gz\
This data stays on your device and is never shared.

Third-Party Services

AutoClic does not integrate with any third-party service, SDK, or API.

Children

AutoClic is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
